Explanation of my top 3 choices

I lived in Yellowstone National Park, so of course I have to choose this as my favorite. I have lots of memories here and will forever condier it a second home. It has phenomenal wildlife, waterfalls, geothermal features, and so many unique landmarks you won't see anywhere else.

Glacier is my second favorite. I backpacked here once, and the thick, beautiful forests and mountains and lakes are wonderful. It is so insanely beautiful.

The tetons would be my third favorite. I also backpacked here and have done some mountaineering. The mountains are absolutely insane and such a sight to see.
