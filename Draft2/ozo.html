Instant Coffee

Take OZO Coffee wherever life leads you.

Our hand-roasted beans are brewed in small batches, then carefully freeze-dried to capture all the depth and character of your favorite OZO cup — no equipment needed.

Just add hot or cold water, stir, and sip. Smooth, balanced, and ready for wherever your day begins — from the trailhead to your kitchen counter.

Instant Coffee

Never run out of great coffee.

Keep your mornings flowing with freshly roasted OZO Coffee delivered straight to your door.

Choose your favorite blend or explore a rotating selection of our single origins — each roasted to highlight the unique flavor and character of every bean.

Delivered on your schedule — weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly — for your home, office, or anywhere coffee keeps you inspired.

Fresh, flexible, and crafted with care in Boulder, Colorado.

About OZO Coffee

OZO Coffee is a coffee roaster & retail operator based in Boulder, Colorado with Espresso Cafe locations in Boulder and Longmont, Colorado.

Founded in 2007, OZO Coffee has based its growth on core principles of service, quality, community, and sustainability.

We are grateful for the opportunity to share our passion for coffee with you.

JUSTIN HARTMAN, OZO FOUNDER
